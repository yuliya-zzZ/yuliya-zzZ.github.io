### About Me
I am an MA candidate in General Psychology at New York University (**GPA 3.94/4.0**), specializing in **cognitive psychology**, **human-AI interaction**, **Theory of Mind**, and **computational social cognition**.  

My research bridges psychology, linguistics, and AI to study deception detection, cultural bias in large language models, emotional dynamics on social media, and how humans perceive AI creativity.

Previously, I earned my **B.Ed. in Applied Psychology** from Dali University (**GPA 3.74/4.0**) with multiple provincial scholarships and merit awards. I lead independent projects, design experiments (E-Prime, EEG, Qualtrics), and apply advanced methods including NLP (LIWC, VADER, RoBERTa), SEM, mixed-effects models, and meta-analysis.

### Research Interests
- 🧠 **Theory of Mind** in humans and LLMs  
- 🔍 Deception detection via psycholinguistic & ToM features  
- 🌍 Cultural bias in AI social cognition evaluations  
- 🎨 Human perception of AI creativity and aesthetics  
- 📈 Temporal NLP modeling of emotion and depression risk  
- ⚖️ AI ethics and computational approaches to psychology
